Equ AI Lab

Engineering intelligence for human health and longevity.

Equ AI Lab is a research-driven technology company developing the next generation of digital health systems — intelligent lifestyle companions that understand, adapt, and enhance human well-being through data, computation, and design.

Our foundation is computer science. We combine advanced AI research, distributed systems, and human-centered engineering to create robust, scalable platforms for personalized healthcare and continuous wellness monitoring.

We aim to build the most advanced health companion — a system capable of contextual reasoning, proactive care, and real-time physiological insight, grounded in scientific precision and ethical responsibility.

Our work bridges intelligence and biology. We approach digital health as a frontier in computing — one that requires excellence in algorithms, systems design, and interdisciplinary collaboration across medicine, neuroscience, and AI safety.

We are assembling a small team of exceptional AI engineers, researchers, medical doctors and designers to define the technological core of intelligent healthcare.
