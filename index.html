Equ AI Lab develops next-generation digital health technologies designed to empower individuals in their daily lives — from intelligent lifestyle companions to AI-driven healthcare systems that promote long-term wellness and preventive care.

We believe that meaningful innovation in healthcare requires responsibility, empathy, and precision. Our approach integrates rigorous research with ethical AI development to ensure our technologies serve humanity with care and transparency.

We’re building a focused team of engineers, researchers, and designers dedicated to redefining how AI supports human health and lifestyle.
